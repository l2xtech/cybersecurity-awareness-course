Frequently Asked Questions about ISO 27001

What exactly is ISO 27001?

ISO 27001 is an international standard that provides a framework for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an information security management system (ISMS). It helps organizations protect their information assets in a systematic and cost-effective way through the adoption of an appropriate set of controls.

Why is our company implementing ISO 27001?

We're implementing ISO 27001 to:

  • Better protect our sensitive information and that of our clients
  • Demonstrate our commitment to information security to clients and partners
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our overall security posture and reduce the risk of breaches
  • Create a culture of security awareness throughout the organization

What does ISO 27001 mean for me in my daily work?

ISO 27001 means you'll need to follow established security policies and procedures, such as proper password management, secure handling of sensitive information, and reporting security incidents. It's about incorporating security best practices into your daily routine. The training modules provide specific guidance for your role.

Will ISO 27001 make my job more difficult?

While ISO 27001 introduces some new procedures, it's designed to integrate smoothly into existing workflows. Initially, there might be some adjustment as you learn new practices, but these become routine over time. The benefits—such as fewer security incidents and better protection of information—outweigh any temporary inconvenience.

What happens if I don't follow ISO 27001 requirements?

Following ISO 27001 requirements is part of our company policies. Non-compliance could lead to security incidents, data breaches, or audit findings. More importantly, it could put company and client information at risk. If you're unsure about any requirement, please ask your manager or the security team for guidance.

How will we know if our ISO 27001 implementation is successful?

Success will be measured through several indicators:

  • Reduction in security incidents
  • Successful completion of internal and external audits
  • Increased security awareness among employees
  • Improved ability to detect and respond to security threats
  • Positive feedback from clients and partners regarding our security practices

Who do I contact if I have questions about ISO 27001?

If you have questions about ISO 27001 or our information security practices, you can contact:

  • Your direct manager
  • The Information Security team
  • The ISO 27001 implementation team
  • The IT Help Desk, who can direct your query to the appropriate person
