Welcome to Cybersecurity Awareness Training

This comprehensive training program will help you understand the importance of cybersecurity and how to protect yourself and your organization from cyber threats.

The course consists of 10 modules, each followed by a quiz to test your knowledge. Upon completion of all modules and quizzes, you will receive a personalized certificate.

Course Modules:

  1. Introduction to Information Security
  2. Common Cyber Threats and Attacks
  3. Password Security and Authentication
  4. Email and Communication Security
  5. Safe Internet Browsing
  6. Mobile Device and Remote Work Security
  7. Data Protection and Privacy
  8. Document and File Security
  9. Company Security Policies
  10. Incident Reporting and Response
