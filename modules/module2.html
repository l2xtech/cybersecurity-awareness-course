Module 2: Common Cyber Threats

Slide 1 of 6

Learning Objectives

  • Identify various types of cyber threats
  • Recognize signs of phishing and social engineering
  • Understand how malware spreads and infects systems
  • Learn preventive measures against common attacks

Types of Cyber Threats

  • Malware: Malicious software like viruses and trojans
  • Phishing: Deceptive emails or messages
  • Social Engineering: Psychological manipulation
  • Ransomware: Data held hostage
  • MitM Attacks: Data interception
  • DoS Attacks: Disrupting services
  • Insider Threats: Internal misuse of access

Phishing & How to Spot It

  • Urgency or threats in message
  • Suspicious sender addresses
  • Unexpected attachments or links
  • Requests for personal info

Social Engineering

  • Pretexting, Baiting, Quid Pro Quo
  • Tailgating, Scareware
  • Defenses: verify, question, report

Malware & Ransomware

  • Viruses, worms, trojans
  • Keyloggers, spyware, adware
  • Ransomware: encrypts data for payment
