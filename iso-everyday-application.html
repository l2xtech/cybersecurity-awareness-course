Everyday Application of ISO 27001

ISO 27001 might sound technical, but its principles can be applied in your everyday work activities. Here are practical ways to incorporate information security best practices into your daily routine:

At Your Desk

  • Lock your screen when stepping away (Win+L or Ctrl+Cmd+Q)
  • Keep your desk clear of sensitive documents
  • Use secure storage for physical documents
  • Be mindful of what's visible during video calls
  • Don't leave passwords written down or visible

Digital Communication

  • Verify email recipients before sending sensitive information
  • Use secure file sharing methods for confidential documents
  • Be cautious with links and attachments in emails
  • Consider if information is appropriate for chat vs. email
  • Use approved communication channels for work discussions

Remote Work

  • Use a secure VPN when connecting to company resources
  • Keep work and personal activities separate
  • Be mindful of who might overhear work conversations
  • Secure your home network with strong passwords
  • Store company equipment safely when not in use

Data Handling

  • Know what data is considered sensitive in your role
  • Follow proper procedures for data sharing
  • Delete unnecessary files regularly
  • Use secure deletion methods for sensitive information
  • Back up important work regularly

Remember

Information security is everyone's responsibility. By incorporating these practices into your daily routine, you're actively contributing to our organization's ISO 27001 compliance and helping protect our valuable information assets. If you're ever unsure about the security implications of an action, it's always better to ask for guidance.
